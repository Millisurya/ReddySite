Implantology

Dental Implants & Full‑Arch Solutions

Most of Dr Reddy’s current clinical work is in implant dentistry. Treatment is planned digitally and delivered with a focus on long‑term function, aesthetics, and patient comfort.

Single Tooth

Replace a missing tooth

Immediate or delayed placement depending on bone and soft‑tissue profile; custom abutments and screw‑retained crowns.

Multiple Teeth

Bridges supported by implants

Segmental prostheses to avoid long‑span cantilevers; staged or simultaneous placement.

Full‑Arch

Immediate‑load (All‑on‑X)

Teeth‑in‑a‑day protocols where appropriate; provisional fixed bridge on the day of surgery; definitive after integration.

Bone & Sinus

Grafting & sinus lift

Socket preservation, GBR, ridge augmentation; lateral and crestal sinus augmentation based on CBCT assessment.

Digital

Guided surgery

CBCT‑based planning, surgical guides, and intra‑oral scanning to improve accuracy and patient experience.

Revisions

Complication management

Assessment and management of failing implants, prosthetic complications, and peri‑implant disease.

Patient pathway
  1. Consultation — history, photographs, and clinical exam.
  2. 3D planning — CBCT / scans, risk assessment, and a personalised plan.
  3. Surgery — placement with or without grafting; immediate provisional where suitable.
  4. Integration — review and soft‑tissue maturation.
  5. Definitive restoration — custom abutment and crown/bridge; hygiene and maintenance plan.

Advanced Implant Dentistry

Complex Cases Only — Referred, Planned, and Delivered with Precision

Dr Reddy’s current practice is focused on advanced implant rehabilitation. Cases are accepted on referral for complex indications and are planned with CBCT and digital workflows.

Full‑Arch

Immediate‑load (All‑on‑X)

Teeth‑in‑a‑day protocols where suitable, with multi‑disciplinary planning and staged definitive prosthetics.

Graftless / Limited Bone

Advanced biomechanics

Angulated implants, short implants, and staged options; zygomatic/pterygoid considered in selected centres.

Augmentation

GBR & sinus lift

Socket preservation, ridge split/expansion, lateral/crestal sinus augmentation based on CBCT assessment.

Guided Surgery

Digital planning

Intra‑oral scanning and guide fabrication to enhance accuracy and patient comfort.

Sedation & Comfort

LA • IV sedation • GA

Sedation delivered in line with SAAD principles and practice protocols.

Revisions

Failing implants

Assessment and management of peri‑implant disease and prosthetic complications.
